About Echona

Echona is a vibrant gathering of music, art, and culture. It's an event that celebrates the power of live performances and brings together a diverse range of artists from around the world. From electrifying concerts to intimate acoustic sessions, Echona offers a unique blend of experiences that cater to all music lovers. Join us and immerse yourself in the rhythm of Echona, where every event is a new journey into the world of music.
