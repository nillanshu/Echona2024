About Echona

A spectacular event celebrating talent, creativity and unity all-encompassing technical knowledge as well as cultural essences. The on-campus biggest festival of the year embracing a vibrant desert carnival theme, transforming the campus into a mirage of colors and excitement.

Pleasant evenings full of sandy hues, exotic decorations and a fusion of traditional desert elements with technically modernized carnival flair. It’s the journey into a festive oasis under the starlit night.

Just-A-Minute is an all round-fun event that is all about the control of the mind over the mouth. So fill the unforgiving minute with sixty seconds worth of impeccable English and you would be the winner. You will be given a line and you have to create a story on the basis of that given line.
